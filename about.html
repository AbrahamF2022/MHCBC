Our Mission

Welcome to Mount Hermon Community Baptist Church! Since our establishment in 1991, we have been dedicated to serving the beautiful community of Sunrise, Florida. Under the compassionate leadership of Senior Pastor Morrison since 2006, we strive to be a beacon of hope and love in our area.

Our mission is clear: To broadcast the Word of God throughout our community and bring others to know and experience God's love through the various ministries of our church. We believe in the power of connection and the importance of sharing God’s message with everyone around us.


Join Us


We invite you to explore our various ministries and activities, where you can grow in your faith and experience the love of God in action. Whether you are new to the community or seeking a church home, we welcome you with open arms.

Remember the words of Hebrews 10:24-25: “And let us consider how we may spur one another on toward love and good deeds, not giving up meeting together, as some are in the habit of doing, but encouraging one another.”

At Mount Hermon Community Baptist Church, we are more than just a church—we are a family. Join us as we embark on this journey together, spreading God’s love and light in our community. We look forward to welcoming you!
